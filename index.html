Some common questions
were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Plants falling off and dying may be due to lack of soil moisture and nutrients among many factors. Keep your plant in optimum temperatures and try to plant during the season.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Pale leaves usually suggest a plant isn't receiving enough light.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Brown leaves are often related to over-exposure to the sun

How do i choose a plant?

Choose a plant according to the nature of location, season, temperature and be determined to take care of it.

How do I change the pots?

Remove plant from current pot, replace old potting mix with new one, and water it.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

A fungus gnat infestation usually happens when there's too much moisture in the soil.
